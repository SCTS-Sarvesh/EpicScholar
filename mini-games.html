Rock Paper Scissors

A global classic simplified for quick, competitive fun.

Game Objectives & Rules

  • Choose between Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The AI chooses randomly.
  • Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
  • First to win 5 rounds is declared the champion.
  • The system tracks your win/loss ratio for bragging rights.

Advanced Tips

  • The AI is truly random, but sometimes our brains try to find patterns where none exist—don't overthink it!
  • Use the first few rounds to gauge your own subconscious patterns before the AI can predict them.
🏏

Hand Cricket

Experience the excitement of cricket, simplified to a digital single-player version.

Game Objectives & Rules

  • Choose a number (1 to 6) for batting or bowling.
  • When batting: Your number is added to your score.
  • When bowling: If you choose the same number as the AI batsman, they are OUT.
  • One wicket per innings. The chase begins after the first innings.
  • Win by successfully chasing the target score set by the AI.

Advanced Tips

  • Pay attention to number probability; the AI tends to stick to smaller or larger numbers in sequences.
  • Use random sequences to deceive the AI when bowling.
♟️

Chess

A timeless game of strategy and tactics against a challenging AI.

Game Objectives & Rules

  • Objective: Checkmate the opponent's King.
  • Pieces move according to standard rules (Rooks straight, Bishops diagonal, Knights 'L').
  • Castling and En Passant rules apply.
  • The AI difficulty is set to an intermediate level for a decent challenge.

Advanced Tips

  • Focus on central control early in the game (the first 10-15 moves).
  • Develop pieces quickly and maintain King safety throughout the match.
